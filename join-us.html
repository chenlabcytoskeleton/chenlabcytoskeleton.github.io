We are looking for motivated, curious, collaborative individuals to join our team.

We value rigorous science, a mutually respectful environment, and work-life balance.

Current openings

Research Technician: Please email Jiayi Chen (jiayi.chen@osumc.edu) with your CV, a cover letter detailing your skillset and research experience, and contact info for three references.
Postdoctoral fellow: Please email Jiayi Chen with your CV, a cover letter detailing your research experience, interest and career goals, and contact info for three references.
Graduate students: We currently accept graduate students through the OSBP, BSGP, MCDB and the Biophysics programs. Interested students are encouraged to contact Jiayi Chen directly about rotation opportunities.
Undergraduate students: We would love to train the next generation of scientists! However, to truly learn and grow, you will need to invest time and effort. We require a commitment of at least 10 hours per week for a minimum of two semesters per year. Want to get your feet wet in real science? Contact Jiayi Chen for openings.
